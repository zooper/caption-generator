Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

AI Caption Studio collects and processes the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication
  • Images: Photos you upload for caption generation
  • Social Media Data: Access tokens for connected social media accounts (Instagram, Mastodon, LinkedIn, Pixelfed)
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service for analytics and improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate AI-powered captions and hashtags for your images
  • Post content to your connected social media accounts (with your permission)
  • Provide customer support and improve our services
  • Authenticate your account and maintain security

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Social Media Platforms: When you authorize posting to Instagram, Mastodon, LinkedIn, or Pixelfed
  • AI Services: Images are processed by OpenAI for caption generation (subject to their privacy policy)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Encrypted databases and secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • Images are processed temporarily and not permanently stored unless you choose to save them

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of data processing (which may limit service functionality)

6. Instagram Integration

When you connect your Instagram account:

  • We access your Instagram business account with your explicit permission
  • We can post content on your behalf only when you initiate it
  • Your Instagram access token is stored securely and encrypted
  • You can disconnect Instagram access at any time from settings

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Basic analytics to improve our service

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@jonsson.io
